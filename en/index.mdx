## What is PixDeliver?

PixDeliver is a professional platform for photographers that makes it easy to deliver high-quality photos to your clients. Instead of sending photos by email or on USB drives, you can create elegant online galleries that are password protected and accessible through a dedicated link.

## For photographers

PixDeliver was built with professional and semi-professional photographers in mind — those who need:

- **A simple way to deliver photos** - no compression, no loss of quality, and no email size limits
- **A professional presentation** - elegant galleries with your own branding
- **Security and control** - password protection, activity tracking, and access control
- **Effective communication with clients** - selection forms and automatic notifications
- **Affordable costs** - pay only for active galleries, with no monthly subscriptions

## For clients

Your clients get a simple, intuitive interface for:

- Browsing high-quality photos
- Downloading individual photos or entire galleries
- Choosing their favorite photos through a selection form
- Accessing galleries from any device (computer, tablet, phone)
