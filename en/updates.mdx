Keep up with the most important changes in PixDeliver. We publish new features, improvements, and fixes here as they happen.

<Update label="June 2026">
  **New**

  - **Custom domain** - share galleries under your own domain. A simplified setup thanks to Domain Connect, plus dedicated error pages.
  - **Passkey sign-in** - fast and secure password-free login.
  - **AI-generated gallery descriptions** - automatically create gallery descriptions from templates.
  - **Delivering photos to multiple recipients** - send selected photos to different people from a single gallery.
  - **German language** - the interface is now also available in German.

  **Improvements**

  - **Step-by-step gallery wizard** - create galleries in a clear, multi-step form.
  - **Faster uploads** - up to 6 photos uploaded in parallel.
  - **Live shared galleries** - the view updates automatically, with no need to refresh the page.
  - **Selling individual photos** - unlocking purchased watermarked photos and tidying up the locks when a gallery is closed again.
  - **Photos visible after processing** - the gallery only makes photos available once processing is complete.
  - **Studio plan: 500 GB** - an increased storage limit.
  - **Email verification** when creating an account.
  - Numerous improvements to photo selections.

  **Fixes**

  - A security audit and bug fixes.
</Update>

<Update label="May 2026">
  **New**

  - **A fresh look for the app** - a complete redesign of the interface.
  - **Light theme in public galleries** - your clients can now browse photos in a light theme too.
  - **Payment history** - a view of payments for galleries and your account.

  **Improvements**

  - **Nicer link previews** - Open Graph image generation and SEO improvements when sharing.
  - File names that are too long are now automatically shortened on upload.
  - Protection against accidental file deletion, plus better interface contrast.
  - Email message translations.
  - An update to the pricing and plans sections.
</Update>

<Update label="April 2026">
  **New**

  - **Sign in with Google** - quick account creation and login.
  - **Gallery cover photo** - set a cover from any photo in the gallery.
  - **Studio plan** - a new name and more consistent paid plans.

  **Improvements**

  - **Statistics with a time range** - analyze activity over a chosen period.
  - **Grace period when deleting an account** - time to change your mind before permanent deletion.
  - Gallery expiration information now visible on subpages.
  - Notifications shown as convenient messages (toasts).
  - Additional rules and validation for public galleries.
</Update>

<Update label="March 2026">
  **Improvements**

  - A notice when someone tries to download photos from an unpaid gallery.
  - Analytics improvements and fixes.
</Update>

<Update label="January 2026">
  **New**

  - **Notifications for expiring galleries** - automatic reminders before a gallery expires.

<Update label="PixDeliver launch - December 2025">
  PixDeliver has launched - a platform for delivering and selling photo galleries. In the first version, we made available:

  - **Online galleries** - create unlimited galleries and share them with clients via a link.
  - **Photo uploads** - upload whole folders; up to 3 GB with no gallery fee.
  - **Payments and gallery expiration** - the purchase process, a grace period, and notifications about an upcoming expiration.
  - **Controls for unpaid galleries** - download restrictions until the gallery is paid for.
  - **Statistics and charts** - track views and activity on the dashboard.
  - **Account management** - browser sessions and account settings.
  - **Organizing photos** - bulk sorting and automatic generation of gallery addresses (slugs).
  - **Notifications** for events in the app.
  - **Legal pages** - the terms of service and the privacy policy.
  - **A homepage** showcasing what the platform can do.
</Update>
